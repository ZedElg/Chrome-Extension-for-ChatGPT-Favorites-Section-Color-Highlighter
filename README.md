# Chrome-Extension-for-ChatGPT-Favorites-Section-Color-Highlighter
Enhance your ChatGPT experience by organizing your chats with a Favorites section and color-coded highlights. This extension provides seamless management of your sidebar with a favorites section and custom colors, stored securely on your device.
Improve the organization of your ChatGPT workspace with the following features:

**Features:**
- **Favorites Section**: Add and organize your most important chats in a collapsible Favorites section 
- **Color Coding**: Assign distinct colors to chats or projects, making it easier to locate key items at a glance.
- **Local Storage**: All data (favorites and colors) are stored locally on your device, ensuring your privacy.
- **Seamless Integration**: The extension runs directly within ChatGPT, adding “Favorite” and “Color” options to the context menu.

**How It Works:**
- Favorites: Open the context menu for any chat and select “Favorite” menu option.
- Colors: Open the context menu for any chat or project, select “Color” and choose from the palette and if you want to recolor or uncolor a menu item chose the option “Uncolor” .
- Easily remove items from Favorites or reset colors when no longer needed.

**Why Use This Extension?**
- **Organization Made Simple**: Group key chats and projects in one place.
- **Privacy First**: No external servers handle your preferences or data.
- **Free & Secure**: Locally-stored configurations ensure data integrity.

You can download the extension [here](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/chatgpt-favorites-color-h/mepfaoknmcmelkcbhjmobgldhebiphll?authuser=0&hl=en-GB)
